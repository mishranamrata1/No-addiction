About Us

In today's generation, teens are more likely to get addicted to smoking and drinking due to their friends and surrounddings. Even if they know its wrong to do such things but still they can't help but to consume.

After reaching certain age they realise their addiction is life threatening. So to overcome these addiction they need to enroll in Rehabilitation center but due to judgemental society and humiliation they hesitate to go to rehab.

We are here for such addicts who want to contol their addiction. All you need to do is enroll yourself with us and strictly follow the diet, medications and given schedule. Though it's very hard to follow medications on own but, if you want to improve you have to work. First it may take time to follow the given schedule daily but as time passes by you will learn to do so. (Just like how you learned smoking and drinking xoxo irony)

Note: Even if you wish to control your urge it is difficult, it requires many attempts and inputs just to get minimum success.

Services

To contorl and overcome your addiction we provides following services that you must follow if you want to improve.

Diet to follow

Weekly diet chat will be provided on the basis of your consumption.

Medication Chart

Medication chart will be prescribed to you by the Doctor on the basis of your daily consumption.

Activities to do at home

List of activities will be suggested, to divert your mind from consuming.

Exercise Videos

Exercise and meditation videos will be provided.
